About the Sneaker Lab

The Sneaker Lab is an up and coming footwear apparel company out of Atlanta, Georgia.
Our goal is to showcase the newest and hottest sneakers, and bring them into the community. We were
founded in 2023 by Oscar Minota and strive to continue our growth and expand on today's fashion culture.
To keep updated, make sure to sign up for our free newsletter and follow us on all social media.
